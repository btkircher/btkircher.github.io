About

Thymeless Treats Bakery is a home-based bakery nestled in the heart of North Ridgeville. Our passion is to create timeless and delicious treats that remind you of home.

Using only the finest ingredients, each pastry is handcrafted with love and care, ensuring a taste that's unparalleled. From classic pastries to contemporary confections, there's something for everyone at Thymeless Treats Bakery.
